    • B.

      Carvings of Scenes of War

    • C.

      Carvings of Scenes of Sex

    • D.

      Carvings of the Raising of Buildings

    Correct Answer
    D. Carvings of the Raising of Buildings
    Explanation
    The City of Qarth is protected by three walls, each adorned with different carvings. The question asks which carving cannot be seen on any of the three walls. The answer is "Carvings of the Raising of Buildings." This means that there are carvings of animals, scenes of war, and scenes of sex on at least one of the walls, but not carvings depicting the raising of buildings.
